# fursal_mobile

Game On. Book Your Spot Anytime, Anywhere with Sajilokhel.

Tired of making endless phone calls just to find an empty futsal court? Sick of arriving at a badminton hall only to find it double-booked? Sajilokhel is Nepal’s premier digital sports ecosystem, designed to take the hassle out of playing the sports you love.

Whether you are a casual player looking for a weekend match or a competitive athlete seeking regular training ground, our platform connects you directly to the best sports facilities across the country.

Why Choose Sajilokhel?
Real-Time Availability: No more "calling to check." View live slots for Futsal, Cricket, Badminton, and Basketball in real-time.

Instant & Secure Booking: Pick your preferred time and pay instantly using eSewa. Your booking is confirmed the second you finish the transaction.

Interactive Venue Map: Powered by OpenStreetMap, our live map spots your location and shows you every available ground nearby. Filter by price, rating, and specific facilities (like showers or parking).

Player Profiles & Reputation: Build your sports resume. Show up, play fair, and earn a reputation within the community. You can also rate venues to help fellow players find the best spots.

Built for Speed: Developed with Next.js, our web app provides a lightning-fast experience, ensuring you spend less time scrolling and more time playing.

For Venue Owners
Sajilokhel isn't just for players; it’s a powerful management tool for venue owners. Automate your booking calendar, reduce no-shows with advance payments, and reach thousands of active players daily. Say goodbye to messy paper logs and hello to a streamlined, digital business.

Features at a Glance:
Comprehensive Listings: From Kathmandu's top futsal arenas to local badminton courts.

Secure Authentication: Integrated with Google and Firebase to keep your personal data and booking history safe.

All-in-One Ecosystem: More than just a booking form—it's a community hub for Nepal's sports enthusiasts.
